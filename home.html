Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

Are your products approved for the U.S. and European markets?
Yes. We are one of the few manufacturers in mainland China with UL-certified DC charging products. Our EV chargers meet U.S. and European safety and compliance standards.
Are you a manufacturer or a trading company?
We are a direct manufacturer based in Wenzhou, Zhejiang, China. Our factory operates a vertically integrated production system from SMT processing to core EV charger component manufacturing.
Do you offer distributor or exclusive partnership opportunities?
Yes. We actively seek local distributors and strategic partners. We offer a structured distributor program, and exclusive regional rights can be discussed based on market potential.
How do you ensure product quality?
We implement strict quality control at every stage, including raw material inspection, in-process manufacturing checks, finished product testing, and pre-shipment inspection. All applicable products are UL certified.
